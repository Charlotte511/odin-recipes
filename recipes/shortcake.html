Chocolate strawberry shortcake

Description

A crumb crust 'shortcake' recipe topped with layers of Reddi-wip and juicy strawberries, all for less than 100 calories.

Prep Time: 10 mins

Total Time: 10 mins

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 chocolate graham cracker square
  • 1 tablespoon crushed pretzels
  • Reddi-wip® Original Dairy Whipped Topping
  • ¼ cup cut-up fresh strawberries

Steps

  1. Combine crushed graham cracker and pretzels in serving dish.
  2. Top with one serving (2 tablespoons) Reddi-wip, strawberries and another serving Reddi-wip.
  3. Serve immediately.
